INTRODUCING ZIGZEG

We at Zigzeg are online users as well. We know very well that the web is huge and at times it gets very difficult to find quality information about ‘stuffs’ in a city.

With this in mind, Zigzeg is designed to allow people living in a city access to a city’s ever-changing and every-growing cityscape. Zigzeg combines an interactive urban map and a growing-by-day city information network to give people living in modern fast-paced cities insight into the constant changes, updates & happenings that are occurring in their cities.

Look forward to more products, services & feature from us at Zigzeg as we continually update & improve our ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ and strive to literally put the city @ your fingertips!
