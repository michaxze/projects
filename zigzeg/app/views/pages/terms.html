  • What are ‘Shouts’?

    ‘Shouts’ is a feature which allows you to post quick and short updates to your Zigzeg Place, Offer or Event page. It allows you to update people viewing your pages on anything you would like them know in real time.

  • Can I list the products and/or services which my company offers?

    Yes, you can use our Zigzeg Gallery for Places feature to list all your product, services, location and even images of brands you carry. Zigzeg Gallery allows you to create a name, a description and select an image type for each of your image. The Zigzeg Gallery for Places can be accessed via your account page in the ‘My Place’ tab.

  • Is the Zigzeg Gallery feature available for Offers and Events as well?

    Yes, each Zigzeg Offer and Zigzeg Event which you create will have its own gallery.

  • What can I use Zigzeg Offers for?

    You can use Zigzeg Offers to post any of your discounts, promotions, special packages etc. which offers your customers products or services at a discounted rate.

  • What can I use Zigzeg Events for?

    You can use Zigzeg Offers to post any event or happenings which your business will be holding. An event may be a concert, a sporting event, a seminar, a talk, a fair, a convention, a public product launch etc. Basically anything that is for public consumption and requires people to attend, be it for free or paid.

  • What is Zigzeg ZEUS?

    ZEUS is an acronym for ‘Zigzeg End-user Statistics’. It tells you how many views or viewers your pages are getting. It also tells you which of your pages are garnering more views and helps you identify messages/promotions that are effective from those that are not. Zigzeg for Business Free account uses a simplified version of ZEUS whereas Basic and Premium accounts will have access to an advanced version of ZEUS.

  • What is a CCO?

    A CCO is an acronym for “Customer Care Officer” and is a person assigned to handle a particular Zigzeg for Business Premium account. A CCO will provide a Premium account with Premium Customer Care service and Full Data service.

  • What is Premium Customer Care Service?

    Premium Customer Care Service is only available to Zigzeg for Business Premium accounts. A Zigzeg Customer Care Officer is assigned to the premium account and personally handles all customer-care related service of that account.

  • What is Full Data Service?

    Full Data Service is only available to Zigzeg for Business Premium accounts. The assigned Customer Care Officer will handle all data entry, editing & updating jobs for a premium account.

  • What is a “Get us to call you” feature?

    A Zigzeg for Business Premium account can click on the “Get us to call you” button and the Customer Care Officer assigned to that premium account will call the account holder back.

  • Will there be new features and services from Zigzeg?

    Yes, Zigzeg and its development team will continually update Zigzeg’s ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ with new and awesome features and services. We will inform you of new updates, features and services via your Zigzeg for Business account and email.

  • Tracking technologies

    We, as well as other companies, use tracking technologies to collect and store data about your computer or mobile device and your activities on our sites and apps.

    Cookies : When you visit our sites, we place "cookies" (small text files) on your computer to recognize you. We do this to store your preferences and track your use of our sites. Our cookies expire after two years. Ad companies place cookies on your computer to track your activities across various sites so that they can display targeted ads.

    Pixel tags : : We embed pixel tags (also called web beacons or clear GIFs) on web pages, ads, and emails. These tiny, invisible graphics are used to access cookies and track user activities (such as how many times a page is viewed). We use pixel tags to measure the popularity of our features and services. Ad companies use pixel tags to measure the number of ads displayed and their performance (such as how many people clicked on an ad).

    Mobile Device IDs : If you're using an app, we use mobile device IDs (the unique identifier assigned to a device by the manufacturer), instead of cookies, to recognize you. We do this to store your preferences and track your use of our apps. Unlike cookies, device IDs cannot be deleted. Ad companies use device IDs to track your use of the app, track the number of ads displayed, measure ad performance and display ads that are more relevant to you. Analytics companies use device IDs to track information about app usage.
