  • i. General questions

    What is ZIGZEG?

    ZIGZEG is designed to allow people living in a city access to a city’s ever-changing and every-growing cityscape. ZIGZEG’s ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ combines an interactive urban map and a city information database to give people in a modern fast-paced city insight into the constant changes, updates, happenings & improvements that are occurring daily to their city.

    Will there be new features and services from ZIGZEG?

    Yes, ZIGZEG and its development team will continually update ZIGZEG’s ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ with new and awesome features and services. We will inform you of new updates, features and services via your ZIGZEG user account.

    Can I trust the information listed in ZIGZEG?

    You can never fully trust any information provided online (or anywhere else for that matter) without first verifying the information you obtained.

    iii. ZIGZEG User Account questions | back to top

    I can’t log-in to my account. Why?

    You may have entered an incorrect email address and password during log-in or you have not validated your account.

    I forgot my password. How can I retrieve it?

    For security reasons, we do not allow you to retrieve your password. You can however create a new password for your account. In order to do that, you click on the “log-in” link located at the top right hand side of any ZIGZEG page, click on the “Forgot password?” link. You then enter your email address and follow the instructions provided to create a new password for your account.

    I did not receive any email updates from ZIGZEG. Why?

    The emails may have been sent into the spam folder of your email account. To avoid future emails from being sent to your spam folder, please add “helper@zigzeg.com” to your safe list.

    How can I change my password?

    From within your user account page, click on the ‘Profile & Settings’ tab. You will find the ‘change password’ section at the end of the page.

    Can I change the number of times I receive email notifications from my ZIGZEG user account?

    Yes, you can. In your user account’s ‘Profile & Settings’ tab, you will find a section called 'Account Preference'. You can select whether to receive individual email notifications or to receive all your notifications in a daily email digest or not to receive any email notifications at all.

    Can I change the email I use to log-in to my account?

    No, you are not allowed to change your email once it has been registered. You will need to register a new account using a new email address if you wish to use another email to log-in to your Zigzeg account.

    iv. Features questions | back to top

    How is ZIGZEG Offers different from other deal sites?

    ZIGZEG Offers differ from other deal sites in that you do not have to pay to get the deal. It is basically promotions and discounts offered by businesses daily all around your city.

    I like a ZIGZEG Offer I see. How can I get it?

    To get a ZIGZEG Offer, all you have to do is read the instructions written in the ‘How to get this offer’ section in the ZIGZEG offer page. Some may have more elaborate requirements but most should be simple ‘turn up and buy’ type of offers, it all depends on the business itself.

    Do ZIGZEG Offers have a validity period?

    In short, yes & no. An offer’s validity is set by a business that offered said offer. ZIGZEG has no control over the validity period of an offer.

    What are ‘Shouts’?

    ‘Shouts’ are short messages which allows businesses to communicate in real-time with ZIGZEG users.

    How does the slider in the Home page and MapView work?

    The slider in the Homepage and MapView works as a filter to your results. Moving the slider to the 'Place' position will show you all places in your city. 'Offers' & 'Events' work the same.

    How can I get updates to places, offers and events in my city?

    If you come across a place, an offer or an event which you like, you can receive updates from it by clicking on the ‘Zig this’ button located in a Place, Offer or Event page.

    What is ‘Zig this’?

    By clicking on the ‘Zig this’ button or ‘zigging a page’ as we call it, you will add a Place, an Offer or an Event you like to your ‘My Ziglist’. Once a page is 'zigged', you will receive updates from that page.

    What is ‘My Ziglist’?

    ‘My Ziglist’ is a list of all the places, offers and events you have ‘zigged’. It is basically a list of your favourite places, offers & events. ‘My Ziglist’ is accessible from your user account page and you can manage your ‘zigged’ listings from your ‘My Ziglist’ page.

    Can I get reminders to Zigzeg Offers and Events before they start and before they end?

    Yes, you can. If you have added an Offer or an Event to your ‘Ziglist’, you will receive a reminder when an event is about to start and when an offer is about to end. You add an event of an offer to your ‘My Ziglist’ by clicking on the ‘Zig this’ button in an event or offer page.

    How can I recommend a place, an offer or an event to my friends?

    You can share a place, an offer or an event you like using Facebook, Twitter, Google+ or a host of other social networking sites. In addition, you can personally send your recommendations to your friends via email.

    I can’t rate a place, an offer or an event. Why?

    You have to be logged-in before you can rate a place, an offer or an event.

    How can I report errors or suspicious activities in a page?

    You can click on the ‘Report this page’ link to report errors or suspicious activities.
