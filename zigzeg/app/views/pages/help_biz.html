  • General Questions

    What is Zigzeg?

    Zigzeg is designed to allow people living in a city access to a city’s ever-changing and every-growing cityscape. Zigzeg’s ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ combines an interactive urban map and a city information database to give people in a modern fast-paced city insight into the constant changes, updates, happenings & improvements that are occurring daily to their city.

    How can I learn more about Zigzeg for Business?

    You can learn more about Zigzeg for Business here: http://biz.zigzeg.com

    Registration Questions

    Can I use my Facebook account to register a Zigzeg for Business account?

    No, you cannot use your Facebook account to register for a Zigzeg for Business account.

    Is there a free account which I can sign up to?

    Yes, We have a Zigzeg for Business Free account which you can sign up to. Some features and services will of course not be available for the free accounts.

    Zigzeg for Business Account questions

    I can’t log-in to my account. Why?

    You may have entered an incorrect email address and password during log-in or you have not validated your account.

    I forgot my password. How can I retrieve it?

    For security reasons, we do not allow you to retrieve your password. You can however create a new password for your account. In order to do that, click on the “log-in” link located at the top right hand side of any Zigzeg page, select “Forgot password”. You then enter your email address and follow the instructions provided to create a new password for your account.

    I did not receive any email updates from Zigzeg. Why?

    The emails may have been sent into the spam folder of your email account. To avoid future emails from being sent to your spam folder, please add “helper@zigzeg.com” to your safe list.

    How can I change my password?

    In your account control page, click on the ‘Profile & Settings’ tab. You will find the ‘Change Password’ section at the end of the page.

    Can I reduce the number of times I receive email notifications from my Zigzeg for Business account?

    Yes, you can. In your account’s ‘Profile & Settings’ tab, you will find a section called 'Account Preference'. You can select whether to receive individual email notifications or to receive all your notifications in a daily email digest or to not receive any email notifications at all.

    Can I change the email I use to log-in to my account?

    No, you are not allowed to change your email once it has been registered. You will need to register a new account using a new email address if you wish to use another email to log-in to your Zigzeg account.

    Features Questions

    What are ‘Shouts’?

    ‘Shouts’ is a feature which allows you to post quick and short updates to your Zigzeg Place, Offer or Event page. It allows you to update people viewing your pages on anything you would like them know in real time.

    Can I list the products and/or services which my company offers?

    Yes, you can use our 'Zigzeg Gallery' feature to list all your product, services, location and even images of brands you carry. Zigzeg Gallery allows you to create a name & a description for each of your image. The Zigzeg Gallery for Places can be accessed via your account page in the ‘My Place’ tab.

    Is the Zigzeg Gallery feature available for Offers and Events as well?

    Yes, each Zigzeg Offer and Zigzeg Event which you create will have its own image gallery which allows you to upload images specific to your offers & events.

    What can I use Zigzeg Offers for?

    You can use Zigzeg Offers to post any of your discounts, promotions, special packages and so on which offers your customers products and/or services at a discounted rate. You can post any type of offer you wish and include any requirements in order to obtain the listed offer. It is all very open-ended. Do note however that Zigzeg reserves the right to remove or request you to amend an offer if we deem that the said offer is in violation of Zigzeg's Business Service Agreement.

    Will you be allowing businesses to create offers whereby only a limited number of people can grab that offer?

    Yes, this is a coming-soon feature. We are working on a an update to our offers where a business can set a maximum number of people who can obtain the discount, promotion or package on offer. You will be the first to get all the juicy details when this feature is about to go live.

    What can I use Zigzeg Events for?

    You can use Zigzeg Offers to post any events or happenings which your business will be holding. An event may be a concert, a sporting event, a seminar, a talk, a fair, a convention, a public product launch, a competition and so on. Basically anything that is for public consumption and requires people to attend or partake in, be it for free or paid.

    Will there be new features and services from Zigzeg?

    Yes, Zigzeg and its development team will continually update Zigzeg’s ‘Total Urban Awareness System’ with new and awesome features and services. We will inform you of new updates, features and services via your Zigzeg for Business account
